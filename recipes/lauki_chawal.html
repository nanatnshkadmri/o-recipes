Lauki Chawal

Home

🥣 Ingredients

  • Rice- 1 glass (~250g)soaked for about 2 hrs.
  • Water for cooking- 2 glasses.
  • Lauki- anywhere between 300-600g
  • Ghee 1 tsp
  • Jeera 1 tsp
  • Red chilli whole-2
  • Bay leaf 1
  • Moti elaichi 2
  • Salt and chilli powder as per taste.
  • Turmeric powder-1/5 tsp
  • Corriander for garnishing

👩‍🍳 STEPS

  1. Grate lauki.
  2. It can be made in kadhai or pressure cooker just like normal rice.
    Water added also will be same. No extra allowance for lauki.
  3. Heat ghee for tadka.
  4. Add jeera, red chillies whole, bay leaves and moti elaichi.
  5. Add grated lauki, chawal, water, turmeric powder, salt and chilli powder.
    Let it boil on high flame. When water reduces below the rice level, lower the flame and cover. Let it cook till water evaporates.
  6. Garnish with chopped corriander

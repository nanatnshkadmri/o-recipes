Moong Sprouts Paratha

🥣 Ingredients

  • 1-1.5 cups steamed moong dal sprouts
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 1-2 green chillies
  • A handful of fresh coriander
  • Salt to taste
  • ½ tsp ajwain
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1-2 cups atta (whole wheat flour)
  • Water (if needed)
  • Ghee for cooking

👩‍🍳 STEPS

  1. Grind the moong sprouts coarsely (keep a slightly chunky, silbatta-like texture, not smooth).
  2. While grinding, add ginger, green chilli, coriander, salt, and ajwain.
  3. Transfer the mixture to a bowl and mix in the chopped onion.
  4. Add atta gradually and knead into a soft dough (add a little water only if needed).
  5. Divide into portions and roll into parathas.
  6. Cook on a hot tawa with ghee until golden brown on both sides.

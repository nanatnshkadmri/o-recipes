Achari Dahi


Home

🥣 Ingredients

  • 1 cup curd
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • ½ cucumber, coriander, green chilly finely chopped
  • Sendha namak to taste
  • 1 tbsp achaar (with a little oil)

👩‍🍳 STEPS

  1. In a bowl, add curd, onion, cucumber, and sendha namak.
  2. Add achaar along with a little of its oil.
  3. Mix well.

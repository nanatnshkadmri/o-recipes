Rajma Panner Bhurji

🥣 Ingredients

  • Boiled Rajma
  • 100 g rajma (boiled & mashed)
  • 70 g paneer (grated)
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 chopped tomato
  • ½ tsp groundnut oil
  • 1 green chilli
  • Salt, red chilli powder, garam masala & turmeric (to taste)
  • 1 cup dahi
  • Fresh coriander (for garnish)

👩‍🍳 STEPS

  1. In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds, green chilli, onion, and tomato.
  2. Sauté with masalas till cooked.
  3. Add grated paneer and mashed rajma, mix well.
  4. Stir in whisked dahi, cook for a minute, and finish with chopped coriander.
  5. And your creamy, flavorful Rajma Paneer Bhurji is ready!

💪 Macros (per serving):

  • Calories — ~368 kcal
  • Protein — ~23.5 g

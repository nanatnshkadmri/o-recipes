Lemon Garlic Soya


Home

🥣 Ingredients

  • 1 cup soya chunks
  • 1-1½ tbsp oat flour
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 5-6 garlic cloves, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • Pinch turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • Chilli flakes or paprika (optional)

👩‍🍳 STEPS

  1. 1. Prep the soya Boil for 5-7 minutes. Rinse and squeeze very well. The drier they are, the crispier they get.
  2. Coat In a bowl mix: oat flour salt pepper turmeric Toss the squeezed soya chunks until lightly coated. Don’t add too much flour — just a thin layer.
  3. Crisp them up Heat butter on medium-low. Add coated soya chunks in a single layer. Let them sit for 1–2 minutes before stirring. Cook until golden and slightly crisp on the edges.
  4. Garlic finish Push soya to the side of the pan. Add a tiny extra dab of butter if needed. Sauté garlic until fragrant. Mix everything together.
  5. Final touch Turn off heat. Add lemon juice last so it stays fresh and bright. Optional flavor upgrades Parmesan-style seasoning (if tolerated) Smoked paprika Italian herbs Honey + lemon + pepper glaze Peri peri seasoning

💪 Macros (per serving):

  • Calories: ~250–280 kcal
  • Protein: ~24–26g
  • Carbs: ~14–16g
  • Fat: ~4–5g
